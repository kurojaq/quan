Daily Trading Upload

Upload the day's executed trades (CSV). The portal totals the net P&L per trading day and lets you post it as the fund's P&L — the single number every investor's share is calculated from. Trades are also filed in the Log for the record.

Expected columns (any order, case-insensitive): date, time, symbol, side, qty, entry, exit, pnl. The day's P&L is the sum of each ticket's realized P&L.
