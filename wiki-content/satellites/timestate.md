# Summary

A standalone terminal (`timestate.html`, served at `/timestate`) for
Promissory Notes (ZN). It runs a golden-reference **PG derivative stack**
against a **Chronometer Watch**, producing a breach graph with
data-anchored annotations and RIPN tuning.

# Detail

- Modules: `js/timestate.js`, `js/timestate-core.js`.
- Independent of the main tab strip; shares analytical doctrine with the
  [terminal](/terminal/overview.md).
