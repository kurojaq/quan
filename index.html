FAQ

The documentation, in brief.

What is The Qu'an?+
The Qu'an is Hūs Rihtlǽfan's dealer-positioning terminal for options and futures — a set of proprietary instruments that turn raw option chains into structural levels, breach signals, heat fields and regime. It was built for the house's own book first; the instruments are offered by subscription, individually or as the full terminal.
Do I need to install anything?+
No. Everything runs in your browser — the analysis engine executes in-page (via Pyodide), the charts render locally, and the terminal works on any modern desktop or mobile browser. There is no download, no plugin, and nothing to configure.
Where does the data come from?+
Two places. Option chains and greeks come from your own exports (or the house auto-pull, on the terminal), and are processed locally in your browser. Live quotes and price history come from the Qu'an's own gated data proxy at the edge. No third-party terminal or data subscription is required.
Where does my data go?+
Chains and greeks you load are analyzed in-page and are not sent to our servers. Only your account and subscription state live in our database. The optional roaming workspace syncs your terminal state across devices under your own row-level-secured account — and can simply be left off.
How do per-instrument subscriptions work?+
Each instrument — Breach Detector, Field Study, Strike Field, Deep Strike Heat Map, Bookmap Chart, Tri-Horizon Compass — carries its own subscription and unlocks immediately in the terminal. Stack instruments as your process grows, or take The Terminal, which includes all of them (and the Execution Engine when it arrives). Every instrument includes the daily Report, PDF export, live chart & anchor, and the Account Simulator.
What's complimentary?+
The Daily Field Brief — the house read published after each session — is free to the public at the blog, no account needed. Client report links (the read-only client terminal with report, heat map and price) are likewise complimentary for the clients that receive them.
What is the Bookmap Chart?+
A composite view that paints the strike-by-strike heat field behind live price — walls glowing through time, the selected session isolated in color, traded volume as depth bubbles, and a right-anchored call/put profile. It reads like Bookmap, but it maps dealer positioning rather than resting orders — structure, not just flow.
What will the Execution Engine do?+
It routes the read: staged tickets, activation conditions evaluated server-side (they fire even with your browser closed), and a full order lifecycle against Tradovate under bounded risk. It is in active development and will be included with The Terminal and Desk on arrival.
How do trials and billing work?+
Every subscription starts with a 14-day full-access trial. A card is added at signup but isn't charged until day 15 — cancel before then and you pay nothing. Annual billing is two months free. Manage, switch or cancel anytime from the billing portal; upgrades apply immediately, cancellations keep access to the end of the paid period.
Is this financial advice?+
No. The Qu'an is an analytics and visualization suite. It surfaces structure and risk; it does not tell you what to trade, and the Daily Field Brief is research commentary, not a recommendation. See the risk disclosure below.
