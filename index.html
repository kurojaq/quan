FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do I need to install anything?+
No. Qu'an runs entirely in your browser — the analysis engine executes in-page via Pyodide. Any modern browser on desktop or mobile works.
Where does my data go?+
Chains and greeks you upload are processed locally in your browser and are not sent to our servers. Only your account and subscription state live in our database.
What does the free trial include?+
Fourteen days of full Operator access — every view, all instruments, live data. No card required to start. When it ends you can pick a plan or stay on the free Scout tier.
Can I cancel or switch plans?+
Anytime, from the billing portal in your account. Upgrades apply immediately; cancellations keep access until the end of the period you've paid for.
Is this financial advice?+
No. Qu'an is an analytics tool. It surfaces structure and risk; it does not tell you what to trade. See the disclaimer below.
